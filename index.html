How We Estimated Costs

üìä Our Approach

  • Multiple sources per category ‚Äî We looked for at least 2-3 suppliers with published or verifiable rates for each expense category.
  • Range estimates ‚Äî Instead of single-point estimates, we provide low-mid-high ranges to capture uncertainty.
  • Confidence ratings ‚Äî Each estimate is tagged with a confidence level based on data quality: High = Published rates from supplier websites Medium = Industry benchmarks or indirect sources Low = Requires direct quotation
  • Conservative bias ‚Äî When uncertain, we lean toward higher estimates to reduce the risk of budget shortfall.
  • 10% contingency ‚Äî Built into the final budget for unforeseen expenses.
üìã What this means

The estimates below represent our best effort to predict costs before talking to suppliers directly. Think of this as a "prior" in Bayesian terms ‚Äî we'll update it with real data (quotations) in the coming weeks. The goal is to check whether ‚Ç±3,000/intern is a reasonable starting point, not to lock in final numbers.
